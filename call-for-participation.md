### Call for Participation: OpenRNG Project

We’re launching **open-resilient-network-graphs (OpenRNG)** — a community-driven, vendor-neutral effort to create open specifications for quasi-random flat data center networks (ShuffleBox-style passive optical interconnects + expander topologies).

**Inspired by** AWS RNG (arXiv:2604.15261) and Jellyfish research, but fully open and collaborative.

**We need your help** to create:
- Topology & ShuffleBox mechanical specs
- Routing (Spraypoint-like) behavior definitions
- Simulation tools & validation frameworks

**Who should join?**
- Network engineers & architects
- Data center / HPC operators
- Optical interconnect vendors
- Researchers & students
- Anyone tired of power-hungry hierarchical fabrics

**First deliverables** are already drafted (see repo). No coding required to start — specs are Markdown + math.

Let’s make high-efficiency, low-power data center networking accessible to everyone.
